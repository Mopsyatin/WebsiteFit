About Bloom Lab

A calm, boutique florist in Ballina, Co. Mayo. Thoughtful stems, beautifully wrapped, delivered with care.

Our style

Soft, premium palettes and seasonal flowers, arranged to feel natural and effortless. Every bouquet is hand-tied and finished with a clean wrap and ribbon.

Local delivery

We deliver across Ballina and nearby towns. Clear delivery windows, careful handling, and real updates if anything changes.

Freshness guarantee

Prepared close to delivery, stored properly, and packed for the Irish weather. If something isn’t right, we’ll make it right.

What we do

From birthdays and anniversaries to sympathy arrangements and everyday surprises, Bloom Lab focuses on comfortable, easy ordering and bouquets that look even better in person.
